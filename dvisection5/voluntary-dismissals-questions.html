Voluntary Dismissals Questions

Question 1

Why should you refer Petitioner to a domestic violence advocate before allowing Petitioner to voluntarily dismiss their Petition?
Actually, the petitioner should be referred to a domestic violence advocate both to help insure Petitioner’s decision to dismiss is freely and voluntarily made, and so the advocate can advise Petitioner on services available to a victim of domestic violence.

Question 2

What should you ask a Petitioner before allowing them to voluntarily dismiss their Petition?
This is correct Actually, Petitioner should be asked if he or she is in fear of Respondent, whether his or her decision to dismiss the Petition was made freely and voluntarily, and whether he or she felt forced to dismiss the Petition.
