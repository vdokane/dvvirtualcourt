Question 4

Is alimony or child support awarded in a DV case temporary or permanent?
This is correct. Pursuant to s. 741.30(6), support ordered in a DV hearing remains in effect until the injunction expires or an order is entered in a pending or subsequent civil action or proceeding affecting child support.

Question 6

Can an Anger Management class be substituted for Batterer's Intervention Program?
Actually, Anger Management should never be substituted for a BIP unless there is no intimate relationship between the parties, such as repeat violence or stalking. Anger Management classes do not sufficiently address issues such as power and control which are integral to domestic violence.
