Alabama Injunction

Please read the Alabama Order in order to answer the question below.

Question

Should the Court look further into whether Anna Martin's out of state injunction is still in effect?
This is correct. Pursuant to the Violence Against Women Act, 18 U.S.C. §2265, all states must give full faith and credit to injunctions, restraining orders and orders of protection against domestic violence that meet the federal definition if the respondent was given notice and an opportunity to be heard. §741.315(2), Florida Statutes, also states that an injunction for protection against domestic violence issued by a court of a foreign state must be accorded full faith and credit by the courts of this state and enforced by a law enforcement agency as if it were the order of a Florida court issued under §§741.30, 741.31, 784.046, or 784.047, Florida Statutes. In this case the court may recognize the Alabama injunction as a prior injunction; however, no action is required since the injunction has expired and is no longer enforceable against the respondent.
