Question 2

Under what circumstances may Respondent retain possession of firearms and ammunition?
This is correct. Respondent may keep firearms and ammunition if the injunction is for repeat, dating, or sexual violence and the court has not ordered Respondent to surrender firearms or ammunition; or Respondent falls under the exemption granted by s. 790.233.(However, the exemption granted by s. 790.233 does not prohibit the court from requiring Respondent to surrender personal firearms and ammunition.)
